Urban Balance:A Smart City Load Management System

A smart city platform that helps citizens, policymakers, and corporate leaders make better decisions through migration insights, urban planning support, and city-based analysis.

Get Started Admin Portal

Why this project matters

  • Helps citizens choose better living opportunities
  • Supports policymakers with migration and infrastructure insights
  • Helps companies understand growth and relocation possibilities
  • Promotes smarter, more balanced urban development

Who Can Use This Platform?

Our system is designed for three important user groups.

Citizens

Citizens can share their living conditions, commute experience, and city preferences to receive better urban recommendations and support future planning.

Policymakers

Policymakers can monitor migration patterns, infrastructure pressure, and city development trends for smarter public decision-making.

Corporate Leaders

Corporate leaders can analyze workforce movement, business growth, infrastructure quality, and relocation opportunities.

Main Features

Powerful functions that make the platform useful and future ready.

Role Based Dashboards

Separate dashboards for citizens, policymakers, and corporate leaders.

Questionnaire System

Collects structured data from different users for analysis and planning.

Migration Insights

Tracks movement between cities and its impact on urban balance.

Smart Planning Support

Supports future city planning and better development strategies.

Corporate Analysis

Helps businesses understand workforce and city-based growth conditions.

Scalable Project Design

Can later include AI predictions, charts, and recommendation systems.
